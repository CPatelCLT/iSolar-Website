about us

Decades of experience in energy

Measurement Controls, Inc. (www.measurementcontrols.com) entered into a new energy sector: solar power. We bring quality of experience in dealing with energy and people. Our passion is to improve process, product, and people’s life by introducing new technology. Our mission is to help customers eliminate their electric energy bill and bring more people on board in combating Climate Change.

We make going solar easy and translate abstract technical and financial terms into concrete meaning with common sense understanding. We offer systems with entry level to advance upgrades that can be scalable to meet future needs. The higher end options can be EV charging, battery backup or hybrid, and energy management.

Eco-Friendly

Solar energy is Green power and offers the highest environmental benefit. Customers often buy green power for its zero emissions profile and carbon footprint reduction benefits. It is also Renewable energy because it restores itself over short periods of time and do not diminish.

Professional & expert

Our design offers the optimum power density and generation for every project. Offers full service from designing and installation to tax planning complying with various Standards and Codes. Engineering, Procurement, Commissioning, and Performance Monitoring are provided.

Affordable

Though the soft costs are still a hindrance, solar equipment prices continue to fall. Tax and financial incentives help make it affordable. It helps our planet and your pocket and it is incredibly stylish. Its cost is very much comparable to the conventional energy cost per watt.

Our Company History

iSolar, LLC. is a project developer for solar PV systems with the continuous partnerships with professionals and experts such as engineers, accountants, and financial analysts. By working seamlessly together, our team completes projects successfully on time.
We came into the solar power field with a mission to help our customers reduce their carbon footprints.

Measurement Controls, Inc. has been in the Natural Gas sector since late 60’s and works with gas utilities, pipeline operators, producers, industrial and commercial customers. The recent environmental initiates and directives led us to evaluate Green House Gas Inventory for our customers and calculate Carbon credits and benefits for using our product.
With determination to increase number of Carbon credits, we came across the other side of the conventional energy sources: sustainable energy sources. Of all these, the solar technology is easily adaptable at several levels such as residential, industrial and commercial, or utility.
This is how iSolar, LLC. came into the Solar Power field (with a different kind of mission for carbon credits!)

WHAT WE DO

We de-risk the solar energy investment. We design and develop projects and craft related financial transactions. Presentation

Residential Solar

Solar power saves you money. Equipment is becoming more efficient, new software is being developed to monitor performance and efficiency. With financial incentives and rebates put together, the overall cost of ownership can be much lower and the benefits keep coming for ~25 years with higher property value!

Commercial/Industrial Solar

Businesses are no exception to energy saving. It will reduce the energy cost that keeps rising over the time. Solar power brings stability to the energy cost in operating a business. Due to several financial incentives for businesses, the Return on Investment can be very attractive compared to any other form of investment.

Special Entities

What else can show their environmental leadership in their local service area than Solar Power? Renewable energy is the best tool to showcase a Special Entities and Non-Profit Organizations commitment to their core goals. In addition to financial benefits, SEs can also monitor their contribution to reducing carbon emission.

Electric Vehicles

Electricity is an alternate fuel. Electric vehicles (EV) are not a new thing around the corner! They were common in 19th century before the internal combustion engines became common for propulsion. Solar electricity is the latest fuel that is used to charge EV batteries. Our system designers consider this option. Electricity from solar energy is free, non-polluting & helps reduce your Carbon Footprint, and save you aprx. $1200-1500 for gasoline.

Energy Storage

Now, the battery technology is advancing every year so you can store your excess generation and use it later when there is no solar generation. We can design grid-tied, battery backup, or hybrid solar system. In general, battery backup system can be beneficial when the electric utility company do not offer beneficial rate structure for excess generation. We help you decide which option can be suitable for your location.

Carbon Footprint

We offer a full service energy finance advisory service. We are committed to enhance our customers’ financial and environmental goals that can be reducing Carbon Footprint and combat Climate Change. Our project analysis reports are simple and easy to understand the financial, legal, utility rate plans, and meteorological data.
